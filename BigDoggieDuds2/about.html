Big Doggie Duds creates one-of-a-kind collars and custom designed accessories for your dogs and you!

Our business began as a hobby in 2015 to outfit our three mastiffs when traditional store options did not fit their size or personality. Local dog owners who could not find that perfect collar for their furry family member took notice and business blossomed.

Today, Big Doggie Duds designs and creates more than custom collars, including:

Traditional leads and leashes:
•  Training leads
•  Rally collars and leads
•  Harnesses
•  Dog sweaters
•  Bandanas
•  Crate Covers
•  Therapy/Service animal vests
•  Dog visors
•  Dog carriers

Our business also carries items for humans:
•  Earrings
•  Bags
•  Key fobs
•  Water bottles
•  Shadow boxes
•  Tee shirts
•  Lip balm cases

Although we do custom work, our prices are not your typical boutique pricing. At Big Doggie Duds, we provide top quality products for your dog without the "fancy" prices.
